# Product Work Log — React / Next.js
## Period: July 2025 – December 2025 (Retrospective)

This document is a retrospective consolidation of product-focused work carried out in the React / Next.js open-source ecosystem during the stated period.

---

## July 2025 — Problem Discovery
- Reviewed GitHub issues and discussions related to Next.js App Router adoption
- Noted repeated developer confusion around:
  - Server vs Client Components
  - The `use client` directive
  - Data fetching responsibilities
- Identified onboarding friction as a recurring developer experience problem

---

## August 2025 — User & Pain Point Analysis
- Grouped developers into common personas:
  - New Next.js adopters
  - React developers migrating from Pages Router
- Analyzed recurring questions and misunderstandings
- Identified gaps in mental models rather than missing features

---

## September 2025 — Problem Framing & Exploration
- Synthesized recurring issues into clear problem statements
- Explored documentation-first solutions over feature changes
- Evaluated trade-offs between simplicity and architectural correctness

---

## October 2025 — Product Definition
- Defined focused problem statements around App Router onboarding
- Drafted PRD-style proposals to improve decision clarity
- Outlined non-goals to avoid scope creep and breaking changes

---

## November 2025 — Validation & Metrics
- Cross-checked proposed solutions against community discussions
- Defined qualitative success metrics such as:
  - Reduced repeated issues
  - Improved onboarding clarity
- Refined solutions based on technical constraints

---

## December 2025 — Reflection & Learnings
- Consolidated learnings from open-source product work
- Documented transferable PM skills from OSS collaboration
- Identified areas for future improvement in developer experience
