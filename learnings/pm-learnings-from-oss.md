# Product Management Learnings from Open Source Work

This document captures key learnings from product-focused work in the React / Next.js open-source ecosystem.

## Key Learnings
- Developer experience is a first-class product surface
- Clear mental models reduce long-term support burden
- Documentation quality directly impacts adoption
- Constraints lead to better product decisions

## PM Skills Strengthened
- Problem discovery through indirect user signals
- Writing clear, scoped problem statements
- Defining solutions without formal authority
- Balancing simplicity with correctness

## Reflection
Working in open source reinforced the importance of clarity, empathy, and decision-making in product management, especially in complex technical ecosystems.
